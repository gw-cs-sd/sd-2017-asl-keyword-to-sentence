GW CS Senior Design 2017

Andy Acosta: MyoSign

MyoSign is a project that uses a Myo Armband, a gesture recognition device, to translate American Sign Language signs onto a user's screen.

Bio: Andy Acosta is a senior at George Washington University, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Political Science. After graduation, she will stay in the DC area, working in the CRM Department of Monumental Sports and Entertainment.
